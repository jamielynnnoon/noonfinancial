Loan Programs

With a wide range of Loan Programs to consider, you can rely on the expertise of Noon Financial Services to guide you through the selection and application process. Your needs, goals, and resources will be analyzed during the pre-approval process to ensure you are on the right path in searching for a new home. Pre-approval by a direct lender is an asset once you begin making offers.

HomeStyle® Renovation Loan

The HomeStyle® Renovation Loan program allows borrowers to purchase or refinance a property and include the repair/renovation costs in the loan with a single loan closing. Renovations must begin within 30 days of loan closing and be completed within 180 days.

  • Up to 50% of the as-completed value may be used for renovation/repair funds
  • Renovations/repairs must add value to the property and be permanently affixed
  • Purchase and rate/term refinance transaction on second homes and investment properties allowed
  • Occupancy and Maximum LTV/CLTV:
    Conforming Loan Amounts
    • 1 unit primary residence - 95%
    • 2 unit primary residence - 85%
    • 3-4 unit primary residence - 75%
    • 1-unit second home - 90%
    • 1-unit investment - Purch: 80%; R/T: 75%
    Agency High Balance Loan Amounts
    • 1 unit primary residence - 90%
    • 2-4 unit primary residence - 75% » 1-unit second home - 65%
    • 1-unit investment - 65%
  • HomeStyle allows for extensive landscaping and luxury items, including pools (203k does not)
  • SFR/PUDs (attached/detached) and condominiums (written approval from the HOA for renovations/repairs is required for condominiums)
  • Unfinished model home/new construction may be eligible on a case-by-case basis
  • DU "Approve/Eligible" Finding required; no manual underwrites
  • Minimum credit score per DU
  • Subordinate financing eligible
  • Fixed rate only
  • Borrowers who own more than 4 properties (financed or owned free and clear) are ineligible regardless of the occupancy type
Conventional Loan

A Conventional Loan refers to any mortgage loan that is not insured or guaranteed by the Federal Government. They can be conforming and non-conforming loans. Conforming loans have terms and conditions that follow guidelines set by Fannie Mae and Freddie Mac.

  • 620 Minimum FICO score required
  • As little as 3% down payment required
  • Available for purchase and refinance of primary and secondary residences, and investment properties
  • May be combined with additional down payment assistance programs
  • LTVs of 80% or higher required mortgage insurance
  • Up to 3% seller credit to buyer closing costs is permitted
  • Available for both Fixed and Adjustable Rate Mortgages and amount varies by county (call for details)
VA Loan

A VA Loan (Veterans Administration) offers qualified Veterans in all 50 U.S. states a federally guaranteed home loan which requires little to no down payment and the following applies:

  • Served in the U.S. Army, Air Force, Coast Guard, Marines, Navy, or National Guard
  • Available to U.S. Military personnel who have served 181 days during peacetime, 90 days during war, or honorably dischargedâ€”full details about eligibility can be found on the U.S. Department of Veteran Affairs website
  • Available to a spouse of a serviceman either killed or missing in action
  • Services available to Veterans in danger of defaulting on their loans
  • Served after September 16, 1940
  • 100% financing with no down payment required (providing the loan amount is under the county limit)
  • Eligibility for VA Loans can be used multiple times
  • Can be combined with down payment assistance programs to reduce down payment and/or closing costs
  • Gift funds permitted
  • Allows the seller to pay concessions towards closing costs and other attributes of the loan as well as value added items
  • Available for single-family homes, condos, PUDs, & manufactured homes
  • Available for both purchases and refinances
  • Maximum loan amount varies by county, so call for full current details
  • VA eligibility can be used multiple times
  • No upfront of monthly mortgage insurance or PMI
  • VA funding fee is required
True Jumbo Loan

A True Jumbo Loan is above the maximum loan amounts established by Fannie Mae and Freddie Mac.

  • Loan amounts up to $2,000,000
  • 700 Minimum FICO score required
  • Available for purchase and refinance
  • Loan amounts higher than $850,000 requires 2 appraisals
  • First-time homebuyers with proof of rental income

How to Apply

Gather the following documents:

  1. Most recent pay stubs for the last 30 days
  2. Last two-years W-2 statements
  3. Most recent two month's Bank Statement(s)
  4. Most recent mortgage statement(s)
  5. Copy of Homeowners Insurance Policy Declarations Page (or Agent's name and phone number)

If Self-Employed OR own rental property, add:

  • Last two-years Federal Tax Returns (1040s) and schedules

If you are incorporated, add:

  • Last two-years Federal Corporate Tax Returns (1120)

If you are a partnership with 25% or more ownership, add

  • Last two years Federal Partnership Tax Returns (1065) including K-1s

If living in a condominium, add:

  • Monthly HOA coupon or invoice showing monthly dues. If the coupon does not have a contact name and phone number, please add this information.

Once you have gathered the documents, email us to begin the application process.
